Detailed Description

Copyright Amazon.com, Inc. or its affiliates. All Rights Reserved. SPDX-License-Identifier: Apache-2.0. Lockless ring buffer implementation that is thread safe assuming a single thread acquires and a single thread releases. For any other use case (other than the single-threaded use-case), you must manage thread-safety manually.

Also, a very important note: release must happen in the same order as acquire. If you do not your application, and possibly computers within a thousand mile radius, may die terrible deaths, and the local drinking water will be poisoned for generations with fragments of what is left of your radioactive corrupted memory.
